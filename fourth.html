All About Dragon Fruit

Introduction

Dragon fruit, also known as pitaya or pitahaya, is a tropical fruit known for its vibrant red skin and sweet, seed-speckled pulp. The most common variety is pink with white flesh, but a less common type has red flesh.

Nutrition

Dragon fruit is low in calories but packed with essential vitamins and minerals. It also contains a substantial amount of dietary fiber. The small seeds within the fruit add a nice crunch and are rich in healthy fats.

Health Benefits

Consuming dragon fruit can boost your intake of antioxidants that protect your cells from damage. The fruit is also a good source of prebiotics, which feed the healthy bacteria in your gut, aiding in digestion and overall gut health.
